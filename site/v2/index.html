01 / Problem

Most teams don't have a lead problem. They have a system problem.

Random tactics don't compound. Tools without orchestration don't scale. Dashboards without operating logic don't decide. The work isn't doing more — it's making the parts talk to each other.

⚠

Fuzzy ICP

You target everyone. You convert no one. The list looks busy, the pipeline doesn't.

≠

Unclear offer

The pitch shifts every call. Reps freelance the value prop. Reply rates flatten.

×

Dirty CRM

Duplicate records, dead stages, missing owners. The system of record records nothing.

∥

Disconnected channels

LinkedIn, email, ads, events — five tools, zero handoff. Leads die in the gaps.

↯

Fragile automation

A workflow snaps in silence. A campaign sends to no one. Nobody knows for a week.

?

Illegible KPIs

Dashboards exist. Decisions don't. Nobody can name CPL or where the pipeline leaks.
